This is a personal blog about cloud engineering and automation (mostly Azure related) maintained by me, Dan Tracey.

I’m a senior platform engineer with over 15 years’ experience in public and private cloud and software-defined datacenter consultancy on Windows, Linux and VMware infrastructure, including automation, orchestration, design, implementation, migration and support. Experience of working in both the private and public sector within Managed Services, Retail, Defence, Home Affairs, Space, Local Government and the NHS.
